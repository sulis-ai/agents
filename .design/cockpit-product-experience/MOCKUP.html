Cockpit — a refined product experience

One product vocabulary — a neutral monogram chip plus a searchable menu — used in three homes: the top-bar scope switcher, a change's product property (replacing the raw dropdown), and the board card. Real tokens.css values, light + dark. Toggle the theme to check both.

1 · The scope switcher — "All vs a product", refined

The top-left control the founder already has, made considered: "All products" gets an everything-tile (not two letters), every scope shows a live count, "Unassigned" is a real scope, and "create / manage" sit at the menu foot. Picking a scope re-scopes the same board in place.

Accessibility, baked in: every product state is word + glyph/shape, never colour alone; the active scope is tick + bold; "Saved" is tick + word + aria-live; triggers/chips are real buttons with aria-haspopup/aria-expanded, menus are role="menu" with aria-checked radios; every interactive element has a visible --ring focus outline and a ≥44px target; the only motion (menu open, "Saved" fade) respects prefers-reduced-motion. All values are real tokens.css, verified AA in light + dark (see IDEAS.md).
