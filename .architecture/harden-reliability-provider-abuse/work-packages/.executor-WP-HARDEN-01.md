## Plan

**Approach.** Harden (Reinforce/Harden primitive) the shipped reliability layer against a pathological provider. FIX1 caps recovery-thread spawn at one-in-flight per session via a driver-owned threading guard (try_begin_recovery/end_recovery under _retry_lock); the manager coalesces a fresh error into the in-flight sequence rather than spawning a thread. FIX2 adds a non-resettable absolute lifetime-retry ceiling to RetryPolicy + a _lifetime_retries counter on the driver that note_turn_cleared does NOT reset, closing the result/error-alternation budget-evasion. Key risk: must NOT reintroduce the WP-007/WP-008 self-deadlock — slot release stays on the pump thread and only when recovery is actually dispatched; the per-sequence wall-clock window still resets on a genuine clear so normal transient recovery is unchanged.
